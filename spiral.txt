Spiral model:
	-> In Spiral model developement of the software is carried out in part wise or iterative manner
	-> In spiral model requirement changes can be handled in 2 ways
		1) Minor change
		   i)  In the same iteration we carry out changes and add new features
		   ii) Minor change desnot impacts the design
		2) Major change
			i)  Seperate iteration is carried out in case of major change and the previous iteration is destroyed
			ii) Major change impacts the design
